About GuardianGo

GuardianGo is a comprehensive school bus tracking system that provides real-time GPS tracking, instant notifications, and seamless communication between parents, drivers, and school administrators. Our platform ensures student safety and peace of mind by allowing parents to track their children's school bus in real-time, helping drivers navigate efficiently, and giving school administrators complete control over their transportation fleet.

Features

Real Time Location

Track school buses in real-time with accurate GPS technology for complete visibility and peace of mind.

Seat Availability

Check real-time seat availability for each bus for better planning and student comfort.

Online Payments

Secure and convenient online payment system for bus fees and transportation services.

Lost Item Tracker

Report and track lost items on buses with our comprehensive lost item tracking feature.

Notifications

Receive instant alerts and notifications for bus arrivals, delays, and important announcements.

Reminders

Set up custom reminders for bus schedules, payment due dates, and important events.

Voice Message

Send and receive voice messages for quick and convenient communication between users.

SOS

Emergency SOS feature for immediate assistance and quick response in urgent situations.
